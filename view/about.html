Steve - Owner/Operator/Neighbor

Steve's engineering education started around 10 years old in a small Midwest town. Always the tinkerer, he began repairing push lawn mowers for people in the community. Living close to an auto parts store had its advantages and Steve began to understand the value of utilizing all of his resources to complete the task.

Moving out West as a teen presented more opportunities for Steve to expand his mechanical skillset with small engine repair in his garage, with many repairs done as favors for neighbors, friends, and family. Even with the busted knuckles and late nights, his Midwest influence always had him looking for opportunities to help someone.

By age 18, Steve took a job at a local HVAC company and had no problems advancing with responsibility. Never shying away from the difficult jobs or long hours, he decided a formal education was a good career decision. He enrolled at a Community College and, despite the full-time demands of work, graduated with his Associate in Applied Science, HVACR. A little while later, while raising a family, he completed his Bachelor of Science in HVACR Engineering and Energy Management (Honors) at a State University.

Whatever the job or level of difficulty, Steve holds the clients experience with the same importance as the job completion.
